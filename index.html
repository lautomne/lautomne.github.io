L’automne

Welcome to the website for L’automne pop up restaurant.

For our last semester at Auckland University of Technology we are required to undertake a culinary project, demonstrating the skills we have acquired over the duration of our Diploma in Culinary Arts.

We chose to implement a French themed degustation for 30 guests, hoping to learn what it takes to pull off a successful event, but also to challenge and prepare ourselves as future chefs.

The name L’automne, (The autumn) embodies the theme of our popup and the style of cuisine; drawing on French cooking techniques, classic flavour pairings, and seasonal New Zealand produce.
